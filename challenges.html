E-Lab Challenges

Our think tank faced six challenges that immersed us in real-world scenarios, fostering engagement with complexity and innovative thinking.

ELEVATOR PITCH

In this challenge, we talked about who we are, where we are from, what drives us, our interests, a fun fact about ourselves, what everyone contributes to our think tank and what everyone hopes to learn from our E-Lab class.

DISCOVER AFRICA

In this challenge, we talked about the Maasai people. We discussed the amazing facts about the people, misconceptions, problems, and their uniqueness.

HELP LAB

In class, we learnt about impacting our community by choosing our cause or adding value to one; in this challenge, we decided to help build a house for a homeless lady where heavy rain destroyed her home.

HUNT FOR TREASURE

In this challenge, we interviewed a small business, and they talked about the process of starting their own business, how it is going, and their mission in general. We then discussed as a group what we learnt from the interview.

LAUNCH YOUR MISSION

In this challenge, we pitched our mission, the problem we identified and researched, our project idea, how it tackles the identified problem, and why we deserve the 10k grant we all compete for
